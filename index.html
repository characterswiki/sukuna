Who is Sukuna?

Ryomen Sukuna, known as the King of Curses, is one of the most powerful characters in Jujutsu Kaisen. Originally a human sorcerer from the Heian Era, he became a cursed spirit after his death. His soul was split into 20 indestructible fingers, each containing immense cursed energy.

Powers & Abilities

Sukuna possesses overwhelming cursed energy, mastery of cursed techniques, hand-to-hand combat skills, regeneration, and high intelligence. Even with fewer than all 20 fingers, his abilities are unmatched by most sorcerers.

Malevolent Shrine

Malevolent Shrine is Sukuna’s Domain Expansion. It allows him to attack targets within a large radius with precision, combining immense cursed energy and strategic skill. This domain is iconic and feared even among elite sorcerers.

Sukuna vs Gojo

The fight between Sukuna and Gojo showcases the limits of their cursed energy, strategy, and combat mastery. While Gojo’s Infinity and Domain Expansion are powerful, Sukuna’s tactical intelligence and adaptability make him a formidable foe.

Fights Timeline

This section covers key fights from the manga and anime, including his first host (Yuji Itadori), the Shibuya Incident, and possession of other characters. Each fight highlights Sukuna’s evolution as a cursed spirit.
